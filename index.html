About Royal Challengers Bengaluru

Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) is one of the most popular and passionately followed franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team represents the vibrant city of Bangalore and has built a loyal fan base known as the "RCB Army." RCB is one of the most exciting and entertaining teams in the league.
Over the years, RCB has been home to cricketing legends like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle—players who brought power, flair, and unforgettable performances to the field. Known for their explosive batting line-ups and high-scoring games, RCB matches are often thrilling and unpredictable.
What sets RCB apart is its unwavering fan support. Through highs and lows, the spirit of “Ee Sala Cup Namde” (This year, the cup is ours) reflects the team's resilience and hope. RCB isn’t just about results—it’s about passion, loyalty, and a love for the game that unites millions.
Each season, RCB steps onto the field with renewed determination, chasing glory while inspiring a generation of fans to believe in bold cricket.
